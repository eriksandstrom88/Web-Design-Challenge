Latitude vs Humidity

There is no clear relationship between latitude and humidity, other than there appears to be a cluster of high humidity in the northern hemisphere between 30 and 70 degrees latitude. There also appears to be a smaller cluster of very low humidity cities just above the equator, which are desert cities in Africa and the Middle East.
