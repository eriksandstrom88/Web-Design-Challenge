Latitude vs Temperature

One would expect the maximum temperatures to become warmer closer to the equator, and that is exactly what id depicted in this plot. It is obvious, however, that the southern hemisphere (all data points plotted to the left of 0 Latitude) is warmer than the northern hemisphere. Since these measurements were taken in January, that is winter for the norther hemisphere, and summer for the southern hemisphere, so the result is expected.
